Industrial marketing concept

Industrial marketing involves one company showcasing goods and services to other companies who then sell to other businesses or end consumers. It centers on business-to-business (B2B) relationships and interactions, which is why industrial marketing is also commonly known as B2B marketing.

Industrial Market Information

Industrial market information includes data and insights about the businesses and industries that buy goods and services to make other products. This information can help businesses understand their competitors, identify market trends, and make better decisions.

The industrial market (also called the producer market or business market) is the set of all individuals and organizations that acquire goods and services that enter into the production of other products or services that are sold, rented, or supplied to others
