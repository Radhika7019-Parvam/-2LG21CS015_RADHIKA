Flora and fauna

The WHO Arabithittu Wildlife Sanctuary is home to various species of flora and fauna. It has large plantations of eucalyptus and sandalwood, surrounded by agricultural fields. Other species of animals like wild pig, leopard, fox, spotted deer, mongoose, etc., are also found there.
