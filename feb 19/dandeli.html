Nature & Adventure at its Best!

Nature is at its bountiful best at Dandeli (North Karnataka), making it an ideal holiday destination. This destination, located on the banks of the Kali River, offers unparalleled scenic beauty complemented by exotic wildlife in its tropical forests.

Get ready for the ultimate adventure holiday in Dandeli

Some of the major attractions of Dandeli include: Whitewater Rafting, Birdwatching, Nature Walks, Jungle Safari, Kayaking, Camping, Visit to Syntheri Rocks, Eco Park Visit, Family Picnics.
